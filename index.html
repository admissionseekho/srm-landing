❓ Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SRM management quota B.Tech admission?+

Management quota is a set of seats reserved by SRM University for direct admission. These seats do not require JEE Main or SRMJEEE scores. Admission is based on 12th board marks, and the process is managed directly through authorized admission counsellors like Admission Seekho.

What is the minimum eligibility for B.Tech at SRM?+

For B.Tech, you need to have passed 12th (Class XII) with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics (PCM). Minimum aggregate required is 50% for most branches, and 60% for CSE-related branches. Students with lower marks can still apply — our counsellors will advise based on your specific situation.

How much is the management quota fee for B.Tech at SRM?+

Management quota fees vary by branch and campus. At SRM Kattankulathur, CSE branches typically range from ₹3.50 – 4.50 Lakhs per year. Fees are lower at other campuses. Please refer to our fee structure section above, or call us for exact current figures as fees are subject to annual revision.

Is JEE required for direct admission in SRM?+

No, JEE Main is NOT required for management quota admission. However, a good JEE score can sometimes help in getting merit-based fee concessions. Under management quota, admission is primarily based on 12th board percentage.

How quickly can I get admission confirmed?+

Once you submit your documents and pay the initial admission fee, SRM University typically issues the official admission letter within 24–48 working hours. Our team ensures your application is expedited to avoid any delay.

What documents are required for B.Tech admission?+

Typically required: 12th marksheet & passing certificate, 10th marksheet, Transfer Certificate (TC), Migration Certificate (if applicable), 8 passport-size photos, Aadhar Card (student & parents), and Category Certificate (if applicable). Our counsellor will give you a complete, personalized checklist.
